Rosogolla

Ingredients:

  • Milk - 1L
  • Refined white sugar - 1⅟₂ cups
  • Water - 4 cups
  • Lemon juice - 3 tbsp.
  • Semolina - as required
  • Green cardamom pods - 3
  • Ice water - 1L

Procedure:

  1. Make chenna:

    Boil 1 litre of whole milk and add lemon juice gradually while stirring gently. Allow it to stand for ½ minute to curdle.

  2. Drain and Knead:

    Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth to get chenna (a fresh cottage cheese). Knead the chenna with some semolina

    until it forms a smooth dough.

  3. Make balls:

    Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.

  4. Prepare sugar syrup:

    In a large pan, add sugar and water. Bring it to a boil.

  5. Cook Rosogolla:

    Add the chenna balls to the boiling sugar syrup. Cover the pan and cook for about 15-20 minutes.

  6. Cool down:

    Turn off the heat and let the rosogolla cool down in the syrup.
