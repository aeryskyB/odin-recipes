Chicken biryani

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - ⅟₂ kg.
  • Basmati rice - 2 cups
  • Yogurt - ⅟₂ cup
  • Spicies - as required
    • Cinamon sticks
    • Cardamom pods
    • Cloves
    • Bay leaf
    • Cumin seeds
  • Edible oil (optional Attar) - as required
  • Herbs - as required
  • Saffron infused milk - as required
  • Fried onion - ⅟₂ cup
  • Garlic, Ginger, Green chilies - as required

Procedure:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt along with spices and herbs.
  2. In a pan, add oil. Once hot, add the spices and sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. After 3 minutes, add garlic, ginger, and green chilies.
  5. Flip the chicken and cook for another 2 minutes
  6. Layer the raw/uncooked marinated meat at the bottom of a wide pot followed by another layer of par cooked basmati rice,
    herbs, saffron infused milk, fried onions and ghee or attar
  7. The pot is sealed to trap the steam and is dum cooked on a low heat.
