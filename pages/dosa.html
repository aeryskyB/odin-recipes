Dosa

Ingredients:

  • Urad dal - ½ cup
  • Idli Rice or Parboiled Rice - 1 cup
  • Regular Rice - 1 cup
  • Chana Dal (Chick pea) - 2 tbsp.
  • Fenugreek Seeds - ½ tbsp.
  • Poha (flattened rice) - 2 tbsp.
  • Rock Salt (edible and food grade or non-iodized salt or sea salt crystals) - 1 tbsp.
  • Oil/Ghee - as required

Procedure:

  1. Soak:

    Soak the lentils and rice seperately in water for 4 to 5 hours.

  2. Grind:

    Ground them seperately to a fine consistence to create batters.

  3. Mix:

    Mix them with salt in a large bowl or pan.

  4. Ferment:

    Ferment it overnight for 8-9 hrs.

  5. Cook:

    Pour a handful amount of the fermented batter in a seasoned cast-iron pan, spread like a pancake and cook it with

    little amount of oil/ghee until it gets crispy and golden color.
